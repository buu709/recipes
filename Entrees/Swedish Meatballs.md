# Swedish Meatballs

## Ingredients

### Balls
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tsp salt
- pinch of pepper
- 1 egg (beaten)
- 2/3 cup milk

### Sauce
- Concord grape jelly
- Chili sauce

## Instructions

1. Mix together ingredients and form into balls
2. Boil in water, drain and place back in pot
3. Mix together sauce
4. Pour sauce over balls and simmer

## Notes
- Can serve over rice
