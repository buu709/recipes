# Snowballs

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter, softened
1 tin cream (fusels)
1 1/2 cups coconut
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp milk
1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients
Roll into bite sized balls using 2 teaspoons
Roll balls in coconut and chill

## Notes

- Yields: 2 dozen
