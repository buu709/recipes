# Lasagna

## Ingredients

- Ground beef (family pack)
Tomato soup
Tomato sauce
Oregano
Garlic
Cheyenne
Pepper
Ricotta cheese
Eggs
Parmesan cheese
Mozzarella cheese
Lasagna noodles

## Instructions

1. Fry, rinse and drain ground beef
2. Mix in tomato soup, tomato sauce, spices and simmer
