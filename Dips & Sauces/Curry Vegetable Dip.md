# Curry Vegetable Dip

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup mayo
- 2 tsp dry mustard
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 3 tsp honey
- Dash lemon juice
- Dash vinegar
- Dash onion powder

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together
1. Taste and adjust ingredients as needed
1. Serve with vegetables for dipping# Curry Vegetable Dip
